# Kickoff Checklist

Use this checklist to launch a cyber risk management engagement and ensure alignment with stakeholders.

## Pre-kickoff
- [ ] Identify sponsor, steering committee, and working group members.
- [ ] Confirm objectives, scope, and success criteria.
- [ ] Inventory applicable regulations and frameworks (ISO 27001/27005, COBIT 2019, NIST CSF, SOC 2, PCI DSS, HIPAA, GDPR).
- [ ] Gather architecture diagrams, data flows, network maps, and CMDB exports.
- [ ] Collect prior assessments, pentest results, and incident reports.
- [ ] Agree on tooling (GRC platform, spreadsheets, ticketing system) and collaboration spaces.
- [ ] Define roles and RACI for assessment, remediation, and approvals.

## Workshop planning
- [ ] Schedule asset inventory workshop with business and IT owners.
- [ ] Prepare threat prompts and industry-specific scenarios.
- [ ] Confirm rating scales and decision rules from the scoring reference.
- [ ] Set expectations for evidence (logs, screenshots, policy links) to support control evaluation.
- [ ] Arrange secure file storage for outputs and evidence.

## Execution
- [ ] Capture asset owners and data classifications.
- [ ] Document risks using the risk register template.
- [ ] Map controls using the control mapping template.
- [ ] Validate control effectiveness with logs, testing results, or monitoring coverage.
- [ ] Agree on remediation owners, target dates, and acceptance criteria.

## Closeout
- [ ] Deliver executive summary and top risk heat map.
- [ ] Review remediation backlog and next steps.
- [ ] Schedule follow-up checkpoints and control validation dates.
- [ ] Archive artifacts with versioning and access controls.
