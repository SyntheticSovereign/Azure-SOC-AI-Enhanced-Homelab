# Reporting Checklist

Use this checklist to produce consistent cyber risk reports for executives, auditors, and stakeholders.

## Executive summary
- [ ] Summarize top risks with business outcomes and severity.
- [ ] Provide trend charts comparing current vs. previous quarter.
- [ ] Highlight major control gaps, emerging threats, and incident learnings.
- [ ] Outline remediation roadmap with milestones and owners.

## Detailed risk register outputs
- [ ] Include inherent and residual scores with rationale and evidence links.
- [ ] Map each risk to business services, data classifications, and third parties.
- [ ] Show control coverage aligned to ISO 27001 Annex A, NIST CSF, and COBIT objectives.
- [ ] Document risk treatment decisions (mitigate, transfer, accept, avoid) with due dates.

## Control validation evidence
- [ ] Attach testing results, monitoring dashboards, or screenshots for key controls.
- [ ] Note compensating controls and planned improvements.
- [ ] Capture detection coverage (log sources, alerting, response playbooks).

## Governance and approvals
- [ ] Record risk acceptance approvals and exception durations.
- [ ] Identify dependencies and budget estimates for remediation.
- [ ] Confirm alignment with regulatory obligations (e.g., GDPR breach response timelines, SOX change controls).

## Distribution and cadence
- [ ] Publish executive-friendly PDFs and data exports for GRC tools.
- [ ] Store artifacts in a secured, versioned repository.
- [ ] Schedule recurring reporting cadence and QBRs.
